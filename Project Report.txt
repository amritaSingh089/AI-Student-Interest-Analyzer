# AI Student Interest Analyzer

## 1. Introduction

The AI Student Interest Analyzer is an automation project that helps students receive personalized career guidance based on their interests and career goals. The system collects student information through a Google Form, processes the data using Gemini AI, and automatically sends a customized career recommendation via email.

This project demonstrates the practical use of Artificial Intelligence and workflow automation tools to provide career support in an efficient and scalable manner.

---

## 2. Objective

The main objectives of this project are:

* To collect student interests and career goals.
* To analyze student data using AI.
* To generate personalized career recommendations.
* To automate email communication.
* To reduce manual effort in career counseling.

---

## 3. Tools and Technologies Used

* Google Forms
* Google Sheets
* Zapier
* Gemini AI
* Gmail

---

## 4. System Workflow

The project follows the workflow below:

Google Form → Google Sheets → Gemini AI → Gmail

### Step 1: Data Collection

A Google Form is created with the following fields:

* Name
* Branch
* Interests
* Career Goal
* Email Address

Students submit their responses through the form.

### Step 2: Data Storage

All responses are automatically stored in Google Sheets.

### Step 3: AI Analysis

Zapier detects every new form submission and sends the student's information to Gemini AI.

Gemini analyzes:

* Student interests
* Branch of study
* Career goals

It then generates a personalized career guidance message.

### Step 4: Automated Email

The AI-generated recommendation is sent to the student through Gmail automatically.
